About me

  1. About

Thanks for visiting my website. My name is Albert, and I am a Free Software and Open Source professional, passionate about programming, distributed systems and Computer Science in general.

I have recently completed my MSc in Computer Science. During the day, I work as a software engineer and researcher at an undisclosed company. In my free time I enjoy contributing to Open Source software and learning new things. I also write technical articles in my blog.

Be aware that this site will detect your browser language and redirect you to the appropriate language: portuguese, spanish, or english (default, and most used). Being said that, not all posts are translated in all languages. Depending on the topic I tend to use more a language than another.

I also enjoy music, traveling, languages, photography, design, to understand how things work and solving problems.
